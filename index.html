Climate Interviews


May 2020: I am looking for people to interview! Please contact me if you're interested!

Welcome to the Climate Interviews blog!

This is going to be a space centering the voices of femmes and non-binary people who make a difference for the climate.

As a woman who studied Climate Science (at UCLA), I've found it difficult to find femme/non-binary mentors in the field, so I'm attempting to create a resource for other people like me who are looking for inspiration and guidance from voices that aren't always easily findable.

If you want to get involved, please contact me! I am definitely looking for interview guests. I also plan to expand to having a Climate Club of people looking to connect with other people in the environmental field, as well as a thriving Resources page.

I'm really excited to start on this journey! If you want to contribute in any way, get in touch!
